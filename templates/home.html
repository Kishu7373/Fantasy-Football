Fantasy Football Tools

Welcome! This site bundles a few quick, practical tools powered by the Tank01 NFL API:

  • Projections — run a simple mock draft off current ADP, then see weekly projected points for your roster. (This is the “main” working page.)
  • Injuries — fast snapshot of injured players (Top-50 ADP scan) and an optional player card lookup.
  • Schedule + Odds — weekly schedule with betting lines; if lines aren’t posted yet, we’ll tell you.
  • News — league-wide headlines first, with an optional player filter.
